The Island

Harbour Island, Bahamas

Affectionately called "Briland" by its residents, Harbour Island is one of the oldest and most charming settlements in the Bahamas. Dunmore Town — named after its founder, Lord Dunmore in 1786 — is the main and only town on Harbour Island and is one of the oldest settlements in the Bahamas. A small quaint village featuring New England architecture, Dunmore was known for its ship building and sugar refinement in the late 1800s.

Only stretching 3.5 miles long with 2,000 or so inhabitants, everything on Harbour Island is just a short golf cart ride away.

FAQ's & Things to Know

A few helpful answers

How do I get to the island once I land?

If you are staying on Harbour Island (including Ocean View), you'll land, then each person will need two $10 bills: one for the car taxi to the dock (tell them you're going to Harbour Island but the name of the dock is Three Island Dock) and one for the boat taxi to the government dock (if you are staying at The Landing it's a short walk).

If you are staying at The Other Side or The Farm, each person will need one $10 bill for the car taxi to Three Island Dock, where The Other Side + Farm boat will pick you up and bring you to the resort.

What dock will be used most throughout the weekend?

Valentines Dock, located on Harbour Island, will be the main dock used for wedding events and the boat day.

If I have a golf cart, where do I leave it?

Harbour Island is a golf cart island, and you can leave your cart parked in front of your hotel without a lock; just keep your keys with you. Guests at The Other Side and The Farm should plan to park near Valentines Dock and Resort, since this is the dock used for most boat transportation. Otherwise, carts are not needed at The Farm or The Other Side as it is walkable.

For The Other Side and Farm guests, will there be shared golf carts?

Yes, there will be 1 extra shared golf cart for guests to explore Harbour Island at their leisure. Keys will be left in the common area at The Other Side, and the cart will be parked near Valentines Dock.

How do I get to Harbour Island if I'm staying at The Other Side or The Farm?

Please coordinate with Melina, who will arrange the complimentary boat for you as needed. This will not be necessary for wedding-day events, but if you have separate dinner reservations on Harbour Island, let Melina know what time you need the boat; you can then use the shared golf cart for your dinner reservation.

Getting to the Island

Flights, ferries & taxis

Fly into North Eleuthera Airport (ELH)

After landing at ELH, check-in with customs and collect your luggage. Outside the airport, let one of the waiting taxis know that you are heading to Harbour Island. The Taxi drivers will know to take you to the ferry dock, and help guide you to where the water taxis are, which depart from Harbour Island every 10 minutes. After a quick 5-minute boat ride you've made it! Grab a Kalik across the street at Jimmy's bar. You're on Island time!

Advanced reservations are not necessary for either mode of transportation. Total cost is around $20 per person ($10 for land taxi, $10 for water taxi). Please bring cash!

Commercial lines from US to North Eleuthera Airport (ELH)

  • United — from Fort Lauderdale and Miami
  • Delta — from Atlanta
  • American — from Miami and Charlotte
  • Bahamas Air — from Baltimore, Charlotte, Miami, Fort Lauderdale, Orlando, Philadelphia.
